Delete Your Account

Recall — account & data deletion

You can permanently delete your Recall account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Delete from the app (recommended)

  1. Open Recall and go to Profile.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are removed right away.

Delete by request

If you can't access the app, email support@recall.app from the address on your account with the subject "Delete my Recall account." We will verify ownership and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile (name, email).
  • All saved items — notes, links, screenshots, files — and their titles, descriptions, and tags.
  • All collections and favorites.
  • All uploaded files and your profile photo in storage.
  • Notifications and app preferences.

Nothing is retained after deletion. Backups are purged on our provider's standard cycle.
